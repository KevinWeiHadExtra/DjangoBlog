Your place for some of the worst opinions in Gaming and more

I personally think I play a very wide variety of game genres from competitive muliplayer games to hardcore RPGs to gacha games. Not only that, as time goes on my repertoire will only keep on growing. I have what I like, but I'm not averse to trying new things.

Multiplayer PvP Games

I'm a pretty competitive person, so I've played a variety of PvP games like LoL, Counter Strike, Escape from Tarkov, and many more.

Gacha Games

I'm not a hardcore Gacha enthusiast, but I have played a fair many Gachas in my time like Puzzles and Dragons, Genshin, Arknights, and a couple more.

RPGs

Everyone loves a good Role Playing Game and I am no exception. From Souls games to adventure RPGs like Skyrim to turn based RPGs like Baldur's Gate, A good RPG is always a good time

Anime/Manga

I've dialed back on the anime and manga trend as of recent but that does mean I don't consume it at all anymore. I still watch some of the popular ones every season as well as my sizable backlog of anime watched and manga read.

Pokemon

Pokemon is my childhood. I've had my breaks here and there for various reasons, but Pokemon has and always will be there for me to enjoy in all its mediums.

And More

I've played many more games that don't fall into these categories and will play many more. I also have various opinions on the state of the gaming space in general that might spike controversy.

See what I have to say

I definitely don't claim to have "good" opinions or popular opinions for that matter. Some might say my opinions are trash, but I think most will at least see my perspective when it comes to those trash opinions.
