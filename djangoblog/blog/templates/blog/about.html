Programmer

I am a Computer Science graduate with a Bachelors and I really enjoy using those skills that I've built up over the years to build projects that also apply to my other hobbies. This little blog project for one, is exactly one of those projects. Gaming is such a broad space and in all the different genres are apps that can be made to help with anything and everything in the ever growing industry. There are thousands of APIs either made by official game developers and also fan made ones that offer plenty of different ways of interacting with my favorite mediums. One such example is recently I have been on a major Pokemon kick and I've had a lot of fun making a little Pokedex app where I can search up different Pokemon and their information. I also want to make a database pertaining to the Pokemon TCG as I have (potentially stupidly) gotten back into that hobby.

Anime Watcher

I've been consuming anime for a pretty long time at this point. Since I was a kid I have watched Pokemon but I didn't really start looking into the medium until high school. When I did though, I dived deep into it. I understand that a lot of the anime I watch aren't insanely well writen stories with complex themes and lesson, but I still enjoy them nonetheless. I do enjoy a lot fo the popular shounen action animes like eveyone else, and I also do enjoy some romance and slice of life here and there. One of my favorite sub-genres is actually historical/warring kingdoms. Anime like The Apothecary Diaries as of recent and Yona of the Dawn in the past. One of the "problems" with anime though is that there is no guarantee that an anime you love will get more seasons. That's why I started reading manga as often times I can get that little more out of the story when an anime just never comes out with a second season.
